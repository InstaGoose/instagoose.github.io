Whats so special about our products?

Have you ever forgot to invite your special friends to your past events? Have you ever felt that you didnt have time to talk to all your guests during an event? Well, we also feel about your frustration and that is exactly what we are trying to address here. Our Event Management starts from resources you have such as the seating plan, number of guests, etc... The seat planing solution we are building will surely revolusionaize the way we tackle event management. It will allow you to design the event space interactively and plan defferent types of seating areas (e.g. family members, close friends, neighbors, etc..) and send personalized invitations to your guests. Guests will be able to responds to the invitations online and wil know exactly the seating allocations for them. In addition, we can also let the guests know other important details (Bar raea, rest rooms, bath rooms, etc...), so that they feel like home at your event venue.

Our Products

InstaGoose Parties

This is for small scale parties (Birthdays, Weddings, etc...) where we need to invite our guests and track their participation. No extra setup is required as everything will be handled from our cloud based systems accross the world.

InstaGoose Conference

This is optimize for managing medium to large conferences with thousands of guests. There can be multiple simultanious events happening at multiple venues. We also help you to manage the relevant tickets\passes for each participant and design individual venues with multiple types of tickets\passes. This version is available in both Cloud and Onpremise flavors.

InstaGoose Corporate

This is optimize to manage variuse corporate events of a given organization. We provide seemless integration with existing organizational assets such as Active Directory, Dynamics CRM, etc... by means of following standard protocols. This version is available in both Cloud and Onpremise flavors.

InstaGoose Community

This cloud based solution is freely provided for non-profit organizations to support managing events with each having upto 100 participants.
